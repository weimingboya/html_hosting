The platform’s global reach and robust analytics integration drive high reliability for both consumers and business users. Minor issues connected to external booking handoffs are not frequent enough to impact the platform’s overall reliability.

Integration & Business Features ★★★★☆ 4.5

Google Flights provides powerful merchant integration, developer APIs, and business-friendly features such as loyalty program support and order management. While ideal for airlines, OTAs, and travel partners, it lacks a dedicated dashboard and full-suite travel management for corporate use, making it less competitive than pure B2B travel platforms.

Customer Support & Resources ★★★☆☆ 3.5

Performance & Reliability ★★★★★ 5.0

Integration & Business Features ★★★★☆ 4.5

Skyscanner provides top-tier API and integration options for established travel businesses, supporting real-time pricing and automated distribution. Customer Support & Resources ★★★★☆ 4.5

Performance & Reliability ★★★★☆ 4.5

Agoda's platform demonstrates impressive technical reliability with industry-leading uptime, real-time monitoring, and robust scalability. Backend resilience is almost without peer, but front-facing booking reliability (especially for refunds/high-risk flags) and airline integration delays expose operational weaknesses impacting customer experience.

Integration & Business Features ★★★★☆ 4.5

Agoda provides a strong suite of merchant and partner features, including robust real-time APIs, commission-based models, and self-management portals. These support third-party and business integrations at scale. Customer Support & Resources ★★★☆☆ 3.5

A. Opting Out of Behavioral Advertising Cookies

B. Turning Cookies On or Off in Your Browser

  1. 5.1 Google Chrome

    • Click the ⋮ menu → Settings → Privacy and security → Site Settings → Cookies and site data.

    • Enable all cookies: Select "Allow all cookies."

    • First‑party only: Select "Block third‑party cookies."

    • Disable all cookies: Select "Block all cookies."

  2. 5.2 Microsoft Edge

    • Click ⋯ → Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Manage and delete cookies and site data.

    • Choose "Allow sites to save and read cookie data" (default) or "Block third‑party cookies."

    • To stop all cookies, turn off "Allow sites to save and read cookie data."

  3. 5.3 Internet Explorer 6–11

    • Go to Tools → Internet Options → Privacy.

    • Move the slider to "Medium" or lower to allow cookies.

    • Slide to "Block All Cookies" to disable them.

  4. 5.4 Mozilla Firefox

    • Open ☰ menu → Settings → Privacy & Security.

    • Under "Enhanced Tracking Protection," choose Standard (allows cookies), Strict (blocks many cookies), or Custom to fine‑tune.

    • Or under "Cookies and Site Data," toggle "Manage Exceptions" or "Delete cookies and site data."

  5. 5.5 Opera

    • Menu → Settings → Advanced → Privacy & security → Site Settings → Cookies and site data.

    • Toggle "Allow sites to save and read cookie data" on or off.

  6. 5.6 Safari (macOS)

    • Safari → Preferences → Privacy.

    • Under "Cookies and website data," choose "Always block" or "Allow from websites I visit."

  7. 5.7 Other Browsers

    • Check your browser's Help section or contact its support for cookie settings.
