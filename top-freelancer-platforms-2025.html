Customer Support & Resources ★★★★☆ 4.5

Performance & Reliability ★★★★☆ 4.5

However, the 20% seller commission is widely perceived as high, and buyer service fees add up for large purchases, diminishing perceived fairness among freelancers.

Trust & Safety ★★★★☆ 4.5

Fiverr implements high-standard safety infrastructure, such as PCI-DSS certification and proactive Trust & Safety team operations, with strong dispute resolution channels and fraud protection measures. However, user reports highlight delays and a lack of transparency in safety investigations and dispute outcomes, which can undermine confidence for some clients and freelancers.

Customer Support & Resources ★★★☆☆ 3.5

Fiverr delivers a comprehensive multi-channel support system, a deep self-help library, and strong community engagement, with enhanced service for higher-tier sellers. Standard users may encounter slower response times and occasional generic support, and buyers/sellers are often expected to resolve conflicts independently.

Performance & Reliability ★★★☆☆ 3.5

Fiverr is stable and reliable for top-rated and Pro sellers, with strict performance tracking and responsive buyer protections. However, overall user reviews are mixed—especially among lower-tier merchants and on platforms like Sitejabber. Enforcement systems can disproportionately penalize mistakes, and support response times sometimes lag for dissatisfied users.
